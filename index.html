    Objectives

    • 1 Empower students to contribute to and benefit from the global FOSS community.
    • 2 Host regular workshops, seminars, and collaborative coding sessions to foster skill development.
    • 3 Advocate for the adoption of open-source tools and technologies at the college/university level.
    • 4 Facilitate student participation in open-source projects to enhance practical learning and career prospects.
    • 5 Build a network of FOSS enthusiasts within the institution to create a supportive, innovative environment.
